Water Efficiency in Temporary Welfare Areas
ID: 1940
To minimise water usage on site, water efficient fittings have been installed in all temporary welfare areas in August 2020. Project implemented by MACE.
- Hippo bags in 44 toilets (36 large, 8 small);
- 19 water efficient showerheads;
- Water blades on 80 taps.

These fitting provide a reduction in the amount of water used while the service provided to the user remain fit for purpose. These facilities are being used by nearly 1,000 operatives each day, generating substantial water savings.
